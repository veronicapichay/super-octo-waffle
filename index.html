Valentine Special

Branzino Pasta

Since it's Valentine's Day Eve, here is an easy recipe for you and your boo to enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Branzino Fillet
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Castelvetrano Olives
  • Spaghetti or any pasta of your choosing
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Parseley

These steps are only for the sauce. Cook the pasta based on the box instructions.

  1. Fry the fillet in a medium hot oiled pan, skin down. Note that you must thaw your fish first if it's frozen.
  2. Cook each side for 4 - 5 minutes
  3. In a chopping board, slice your desired amount of grape tomatoes and olives
  4. After the fish is done, take it out and set it aside
  5. Using the same pan, sautee you garlic until it's fragrant
  6. Add the tomatoes and cook for 3 mins
  7. Now you can add the olives and some brine
  8. Place the cooked fish back and break it down in chunks
  9. Season with salt and pepper
  10. Voila! Sprinkle some Parsley and a squeez of lemon before serving.
